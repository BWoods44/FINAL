Your Path from Obesity Starts Here

Since 2012, Dwight Schrute’s gym for muscles has provided the amenities of a large health club chain with the service and personality of a hometown gym. Please browse our site to find out about our top-rated facility. Come check us out using our free one-week trial pass located on the “Membership Plans” page. Dwight Schrute’s gym for muscles is a proud member of Schrute Farms Beets. SFB is a non-profit trade association representing beet farmers, amish, beet suppliers, and gyms worldwide. The Mission of SFB is to grow, protect, and promote the beet industry, and to provide its members with benefits that will help them be more successful beet farmers.
